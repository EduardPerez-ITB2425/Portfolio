Projects: Information

I'm Eduard Jordi, I had been studying in high school, where my studies have focused mainly on theoretical areas, although I have always had a particular interest in technology. Throughout these years, I have focused on acquiring a deep understanding of the subjects, combining theory with my technological skills. In Project 1, I researched the influence of marketing on buying and selling, analyzing how commercial strategies affect consumer decisions. In Project 2, I dedicated myself to develop my Treball de Síntesi, where I created a website documenting and sharing our experiences during an educational trip, exploring a part of Catalunya. These projects were fundamental for my academic development, especially in the areas of teamwork, organization and communication.

Project 1: Synthesis work

My Research Paper deals with the influence of marketing on buying and selling, analyzing how marketing strategies affect consumer decisions. I did theoretical research on digital marketing, consumer psychology and sales strategies, and conducted comparative interviews between Coca-Cola and a local business. In the presentation in front of the professors, I explained both the theoretical and practical part, highlighting the differences between the strategies of big companies and local businesses. In conclusion, I showed how large corporations use resources to reach a global audience, while small businesses take advantage of their ability to connect personally with their customers.

Project 2: Synthesis work

I did a Synthesis Project focused on exploration and travel, visiting the Marcel Maillot colony house and places like the Empordà, Empúries, the Aiguamolls, l'Escala and the Medes Islands. We documented everything on a website created with Google Sites, updating it with activities and photos of the trip. The project was presented to the teachers, where we explained our experiences and showed the website as an example of our exploration and learning. It was a very meaningful experience, as I not only gained knowledge, but also improved my social skills, working in a team and connecting with my classmates.
